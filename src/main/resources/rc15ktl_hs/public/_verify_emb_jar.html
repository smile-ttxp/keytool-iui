Verify signed JAR file

Workflow

	
	Fill in all required fields
	==> enable action button located at bottom of active window
	
	Click action button
	  ==> Message dialog shows infos in "SUN's jarsigner -verify" style.

Limitations

	
	Not supported signature algorithms:
	
	. EC:
	  . SHA1withECDSA
	  . SHA224withECDSA
	  . SHA256withECDSA
	  . SHA384withECDSA
	  . SHA512withECDSA
