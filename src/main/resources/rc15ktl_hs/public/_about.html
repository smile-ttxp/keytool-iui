  • About keystore viewers

    No password required to view keystores' contents of type:

    1. JKS,
    2. JCEKS,
    3. BKS.

    Keystores without passwords (or blank password) of type:

    1. PKCS12,
    2. UBER.
    ... just click "OK" button in the "get password" dialog window.
